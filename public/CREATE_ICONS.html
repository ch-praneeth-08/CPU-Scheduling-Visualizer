‚úÖ Maskable Icon Guidelines

Maskable icons (for Android adaptive icons) should have extra padding because the OS may crop them into circles, rounded squares, or other shapes. Keep your main icon content within the center 80% of the canvas.

For best results with maskable icons, use a solid background color that extends to the edges, and make sure your emoji/text is centered and not too close to any edge.
